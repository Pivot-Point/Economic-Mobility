About Prosperity Now

Prosperity Now has an extensive history of researching, designing, and testing solutions aimed at increasing financial security and economic mobility for everyone in the United States, and looks forward to continuing to bring all of our approaches to bear on the growing racial economic and wealth disparities plaguing our country

As our work has grown, we also saw value in adding sections to provide design to visually depicting diverse communities, and complex circumstances and concepts for understanding the nuance and complexity of racial economic equity, the racial wealth divide, and racial wealth equity

Our mission

Prosperity Now believes that everyone deserves a chance to prosper. Our mission is to ensure that everyone in our country has a clear path to financial stability, wealth, and prosperity, particularly people of color and low-wealth families.

To advance our mission, we create and support technology that foster an economy that offers an opportunity to those who have not had it before.Additionally, by intergrating existing evidence to simulate the circumstances that can foster advocacy, civic engagement and inspire individuals to build wealth and a better future. Finally, through application development, solutions, and policies, we fight for economic mobility for everyone in the United States.
